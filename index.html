Privacy Policy for Chess Studio

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

Chess Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Provided Information: We may collect information you provide directly, such as usernames for third-party chess platforms (e.g., Chess.com or Lichess) to fetch your games.
  • Media Access: Our app requires access to your device's storage to save generated video files.
  • Microphone Access: We require microphone access for voice-over recording features (if used by the creator).

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate personalized chess content.
  • Save videos to your device's gallery.
  • Improve our AI algorithms and video styles.

3. Data Safety

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. All video processing is performed locally on your device or via secure cloud-based AI processing.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • YouTube/Google API: For optional direct video uploads.
  • AdMob: For displaying advertisements.

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
